MLRIT CIE

MLRIT CIE leads in creating Hyderabad's pioneering innovation and startup ecosystem pre-dominantly among student community through our initiatives on incubation and mentoring programs

To achieve these we conduct various pilot programs like Hackthon, Technical workshops and help students nurture in various technical sectors.

EcoSystem

THEME

AIM: To form an ecosystem where students are in authorities to take and make decisions for the welfare of the organization and students themselves.

VISION: We intend to create a surrounding for students where knowledge can feasibly flow from one person to another.

MISSION: To promote innovation and entrepreneurship among students and help them excel in it.
